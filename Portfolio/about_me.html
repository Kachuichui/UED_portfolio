My Story

  • I'm Xinuo Zhou, and I'm currently pursuing a master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction at University College London (UCL). I'd like to share my story with you all.
  • I majored in Computer Science as an undergraduate. However, later on, when I first got acquainted with the course HCI (Human-Computer Interaction), I felt like I had stepped into a whole new and different world.
  • I'm someone who loves to find beauty in daily life. Even helping others with a small task can make me happy for the whole day. Thus, I thought, now that I'm studying HCI, can I bring this technology into daily life to help people solve many small issues that bother them? Even, I've developed such a way of thinking. When passing by a trash can, I wonder if there's a technology that can help people sort garbage in real time. When walking past the refrigerator, I think if there's a technology that can help me keep track of expiration dates, so as to reduce food waste, and so on.

    • Design Philosophy

    I really identify with the user-centered design approach—where users' needs and goals are at the heart of every decision. I'm a big advocate for iterative design, constantly testing and improving my work based on user feedback. I also love collaborating with different peers, and I believe the best designs come from diverse perspectives and open communication.
