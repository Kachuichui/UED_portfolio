Hi, I'm Xinuo Zhou

A product designer based in San Francisco, CA with a passion for creating intuitive and engaging user experiences.

My Story

My journey in design began with a fascination for how things work and a desire to make them better. I started exploring design principles in my early teens, and that curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Human-Computer Interaction. Since then, I've had the privilege of working on a variety of projects, from mobile apps to web platforms, always striving to create designs that are both beautiful and functional.

Design Philosophy

I believe in a user-centered design approach, where the needs and goals of the user are at the forefront of every decision. I'm a strong advocate for iterative design, constantly testing and refining my work based on user feedback. I also value collaboration and believe that the best designs are born from diverse perspectives and open communication.
